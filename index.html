OPERATOR GUIDE
SKYSTRIKER-BRAVO // FIELD REFERENCE
01 — SYSTEM STARTUP

All three radar systems begin in the OFF state. Power them on using the toggle switches under the EWR screen.

  1. Flip EWR — wide-area sweep activates.
  2. Flip TAR — target acquisition radar activates.
  3. Flip AER — altitude elevation radar activates.
02 — EWR — EARLY WARNING RADAR

360° sweep. Contacts appear as the arm paints them.

▲ HOSTILE

Military contacts. Faster, higher altitude. Prioritise for lock.

● FRIENDLY

Civilian contacts. Slower, lower altitude. Avoid targeting.

03 — TAR — TARGET ACQUISITION RADAR

Steer the crosshair by dragging on the TAR screen (touch) or WASD keys (keyboard).

KEYS: W=UP S=DOWN A=LEFT D=RIGHT
📱 MOBILE: Drag on TAR screen to steer beam.
04 — AER — ALTITUDE ELEVATION RADAR

Vertical cross-section. Align beam to target's altitude band.

KEYS: 8=RAISE 2=LOWER
📱 MOBILE: Drag up/down on AER screen.
05 — ACQUIRING A LOCK
  1. Power on all three radars.
  2. Steer TAR beam onto a contact visible on EWR.
  3. Adjust AER elevation until the marker aligns with the contact's altitude.
  4. Status bar flashes TAR+ALT ALIGNED. Tap LOCK or press X.
  5. Crosshair turns yellow and tracks the target.
06 — AGNIBAAN FIRE CONTROL

8-round loadout under the AER column. With target locked, press N or tap FIRE. Tap REARM to reload all 8.
