OPERATOR GUIDE
SKYSTRIKER-BRAVO // FIELD REFERENCE
01 — SYSTEM STARTUP

All three radar systems begin in the OFF state. You must power them on individually using the toggle switches on the left-side control panel.

  1. Flip the EWR switch to bring up the wide-area sweep. Contacts will begin appearing within seconds.
  2. Flip the TAR switch to activate the Target Acquisition Radar.
  3. Flip the AER switch to activate the Altitude Elevation Radar.

All three must be ON for a full engagement solution.

02 — EWR — EARLY WARNING RADAR

The EWR provides 360° surveillance. A rotating sweep arm continuously paints contacts on the display.

▲ HOSTILE

Military-class contacts. Move faster and fly at higher altitudes. Prioritise for lock.

● FRIENDLY

Civilian-class contacts. Slower, lower altitude. Avoid targeting.

Use the MODE button below the EWR screen to toggle city name overlays on or off.

03 — TAR — TARGET ACQUISITION RADAR

TAR is a narrow-FOV radar. Steer the crosshair by dragging on the TAR screen (touch) or WASD keys (keyboard).

05 — ACQUIRING A LOCK
  1. Power on EWR, TAR and AER.
  2. Steer the TAR beam (WASD / drag) until it overlaps a contact on the EWR.
  3. Adjust AER elevation (8 / 2 or drag) until the marker aligns with the contact's altitude.
  4. The status bar will flash TAR+ALT ALIGNED. Press X or tap LOCK to engage.
  5. The crosshair turns yellow and tracks the target.
06 — AGNIBAAN FIRE CONTROL
FIRE — KEY N / TAP FIRE BUTTON

With target locked, press N or tap the red FIRE button. One round consumed per shot.

REARM

Tap REARM to reload all 8 missiles instantly.

07 — HEADER DISPLAY

The VAJRAPRAHAAR VAJRAPRATIJANA phrase cycles through operational mottos. Tap it to step through manually.
